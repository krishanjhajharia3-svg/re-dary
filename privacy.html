🏠 Privacy Policy — RE Diary

Last Updated: April 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Krishan Jhajharia ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the RE Diary application.

1. Information We Collect

RE Diary is a personal real estate lead management tool. The following information may be collected:

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture to identify you within the app.
  • Contact Data: Names and phone numbers of real estate leads that you manually enter into the app.
  • Google Contacts: With your permission, we save contact information directly to your Google Contacts account.
  • Google Drive: With your permission, we store backup data in your personal Google Drive.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the RE Diary application
  • To save contacts to your Google Contacts (only with your explicit permission)
  • To backup your lead data to your personal Google Drive (only with your explicit permission)
  • We do NOT sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party
  • We do NOT use your data for advertising purposes

3. Google API Services

RE Diary uses the following Google APIs:

  • Google Sign-In: For user authentication
  • Google Contacts API: To save lead contacts directly to your Google Contacts
  • Google Drive API: To backup and restore your lead data
  • Google Calendar API: To add follow-up reminders to your calendar

Our use of Google APIs complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. Data Storage

  • All lead data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage
  • No lead data is stored on our servers
  • Google Drive backup is stored in your own Google Drive account
  • We do not have access to your backup data

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your personal information with any third parties. Your data stays with you:

  • Lead data → Stored on your device only
  • Google Contacts → Saved to your own Google account
  • Drive backup → Stored in your own Google Drive

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. All communication with Google APIs uses secure HTTPS connections.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in the app
  • Delete all your data by clearing browser storage
  • Revoke Google permissions at any time via your Google Account settings
  • Delete backup files from your Google Drive at any time

8. Children's Privacy

RE Diary is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
